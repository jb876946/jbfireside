Where There's Smoke, There's Flavor

JB Fireside started with a spark—a passion for Caribbean flavors being brought to the streets of Killeen that couldn't be ignored. Our Caribbean homey dishes bring people together with bold heat and rich taste. From our food truck to the streets of Killeen, we're blazing a trail wherever we roll.

Every dish is a tribute to our heritage, that passion, that flame, that JB Fireside kick. Whether it's grilled over coals Jerk Chicken or our savory, but flavorful Oxtails, we bring the flames—literally.
